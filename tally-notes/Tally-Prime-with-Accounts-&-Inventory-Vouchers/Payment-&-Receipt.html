1. Introduction

Payment & Receipt Vouchers are the most used accounting vouchers in Tally Prime.

They record all cash & bank transactions — no inventory effect — only update:

  • Cash & Bank Books
  • Ledger Balances
  • Outstanding Receivables / Payables
  • Invoice & Return Adjustments

2. Payment Voucher (F5)

Used when money goes out of the business.

Examples: Supplier payment, salary, rent, refund to customer, bank charges

Accounting: Dr. Party/Expense | Cr. Cash/Bank

3. Receipt Voucher (F6)

Used when money comes in to the business.

Examples: Customer payment, refund from supplier, income, capital introduced

Accounting: Dr. Cash/Bank | Cr. Party/Income

4. Activation & Settings

Gateway → F11 → Accounting Features → Set to Yes:

  • Use Payment/Receipt as Voucher → Yes
  • Use Bill-wise Details → Yes (MANDATORY)
  • Maintain Bank Reconciliation → Yes

Shortcuts: F5 = Payment | F6 = Receipt

5. Bill-wise Adjustment (Most Important Feature)

Allows linking payments to specific invoices/returns:

  • Against Reference → Adjust against specific invoice
  • New Reference → Advance payment
  • On Account → Unadjusted payment (later adjustment)

Enables accurate Outstanding Reports.

6. Payment Voucher – Practical Steps

  1. Vouchers → F5
  2. Credit → Cash / Bank Ledger
  3. Debit → Party / Expense Ledger
  4. Enter Amount
  5. Press Enter on Party → Bill-wise Details → Choose Reference
  6. Narration → e.g., “Paid by NEFT”
  7. Save

7. Receipt Voucher – Practical Steps

  1. Vouchers → F6
  2. Debit → Cash / Bank Ledger
  3. Credit → Customer / Income Ledger
  4. Enter Amount
  5. Bill-wise → Select Invoice / Credit Note
  6. Narration → e.g., “Received via UPI”
  7. Save

8. Use Cases Connected with Previous Topics

  • Payment → Adjust Purchase Invoices (Topic 6)
  • Receipt → Adjust Sales Invoices (Topic 6)
  • Payment → Refund via Credit Note (Sales Return)
  • Receipt → Refund via Debit Note (Purchase Return)
