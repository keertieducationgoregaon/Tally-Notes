1. Introduction to Purchase & Sales Invoices

Purchase & Sales Vouchers in Invoice Mode allow item-wise billing with full integration of:

  • Stock Quantity & Rate
  • Godown Tracking
  • Order & Delivery/Receipt Note Linking
  • Discounts & Narration

They update both Accounts and Inventory simultaneously.

2. Types of Transactions Supported

  • Trading: Buy & sell finished goods
  • Manufacturing: Raw → Finished Goods
  • Service: Sell services + consumables

3. Key Ledgers & Items (Master Data – Reused)

Party Ledgers: Star Mobiles, Techno Wholesale (Suppliers) | Bright Traders, Green Retailer (Customers)

Accounting Ledgers: Purchase A/c, Sales A/c, Round Off

4. Activation Settings for Full Invoice Mode

F11 → Accounting Features:

  • Integrate Accounts with Inventory → Yes
  • Bill-wise Details → Yes
  • Order Processing → Yes
  • Tracking Numbers → Yes

F11 → Inventory Features: Integrate Accounts with Inventory → Yes

F12 (in Voucher): Use Invoice Mode → Yes

Shortcut in Voucher: Ctrl + H → Change to Invoice Mode

5. Purchase Voucher (Invoice Mode) – F9

Accounting Effect: Dr. Purchase A/c | Cr. Supplier | Stock ↑

Steps: F9 → Ctrl+H (Invoice) → Supplier → Purchase Ledger → Enter Items → Save

6. Sales Voucher (Invoice Mode) – F8

Accounting Effect: Dr. Customer | Cr. Sales A/c | Stock ↓

Steps: F8 → Ctrl+H (Invoice) → Customer → Sales Ledger → Enter Items → Save

7. Purchase with PO Tracking

During Purchase Invoice → Press Alt + O → Select Purchase Order → Items auto-load

8. Sales with SO Tracking

In Sales Invoice → Alt + O → Select Sales Order → Items auto-fill

9. Purchase with Receipt Note Tracking

In Purchase Invoice → Tracking No. → Select Receipt Note → Avoids double stock entry

10. Sales with Delivery Note Tracking

In Sales Invoice → Tracking No. → Select Delivery Note → Stock already reduced
