4. Golden Rules of Accounting (Most Important)

Personal Account

Debit ‚Üí The Receiver
Credit ‚Üí The Giver

Ex: Ram pays us ‚Üí Dr. Ram A/c

Real Account

Debit ‚Üí What Comes In
Credit ‚Üí What Goes Out

Ex: Furniture purchased ‚Üí Dr. Furniture

Nominal Account

Debit ‚Üí All Expenses & Losses
Credit ‚Üí All Incomes & Gains

Ex: Paid Salary ‚Üí Dr. Salary A/c

Mnemonic: P-R-G ‚Üí Personal-Receiver, Real-Comes In, Nominal-Expense
