1. Company Creation

1.1 Theory

A Company in Tally Prime is the base container that stores your business data — ledgers, vouchers, inventory, reports, GST details, and financial year information.
Tally allows you to create multiple companies for different businesses.

1.2 Practical: Steps to Create a Company

  1. Open Tally Prime → GO TO → Create Company
    Shortcut: Alt + K → Company → Create
  2. Fill Company Details:
    • Company Name: Business / Firm Name
    • Mailing Name: Printed on invoice
    • Address, Phone, Email
    • Financial Year Begins: Default 1-Apr
    • Books Begin From: Start of accounting year
    • Currency: INR
  3. Set Security & Features (Optional):
    • Tally Vault Password (encryption)
    • User Security Control
    • Enable GST & Inventory
  4. Press Ctrl + A to save

2. Company Alteration

2.1 Theory

Alteration means editing or updating company details without losing any data.
You can change: Address, Contact Details, Company Features, GST Settings, etc.

2.2 Practical: Steps to Alter Company

  1. Gateway of Tally → Alt + K → Alter
  2. Select the company → Make changes
  3. Save with Ctrl + A

3. Company Deletion

3.1 Theory

Deletion permanently removes the company and all its data.
⚠️ Cannot be undone → Always take backup first!

3.2 Practical: Steps to Delete a Company

  1. Select Company from list
  2. Press Alt + K → Alter
  3. Press Alt + D → Confirm Yes

4. Shut Company

4.1 Theory

Shutting a company closes it temporarily without deleting.
Useful when switching companies or cleaning workspace.

4.2 Practical: Steps to Shut a Company

  1. From Gateway → Press Alt + F1
  2. Select company → Press Enter

5. Select Company

5.1 Theory

Selecting a company opens it for use. You can work with single or multiple companies simultaneously for comparison.

5.2 Practical: Steps to Select a Company

  • Gateway of Tally → Select Company
  • Choose company → Press Enter
  • For multiple: Press Spacebar on each → Enter
